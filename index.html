About

Hexacon is a whitelist Survival Multiplayer (SMP) server made up primarily of content creators. We create videos for our Youtube channels, while also livestreaming on Twitch/dLive/Youtube. We make content frequently, and are active on the server quite often.

The goal of Hexacon is to be family friendly, fun, creative, unique and inspiring. We want our viewers to enjoy our videos and feel comfortable while watching them. We want to create new things in vanilla Minecraft that no one has tried before. We hope that our viewers will become inspired to create their own ideas in the game, or even considering playing with us.

Our server is international, with the majority of our members in various areas of the United States and across Europe. We have a variety of ages, ethnicities, orientations and backgrounds. We do not exclude anyone based on anything outside of our server rules and requirements to join.
